AI Integration
What does the AI do?
The AI reads your actual tracking data — which buckets you spent time on, how many important tasks you completed vs planned, your daily hours vs target — and gives you a direct, honest coaching read. It tells you where you drifted, what to prioritize, and whether your effort is aligned with your goals. No API key? It falls back to rule-based logic using the same data.
